## Why

当前关于 A 股游资的公开信息很多，但大多分散在龙虎榜数据页、社区复盘、媒体整理和人物叙事中，缺少一个可以稳定复用、结构统一、边界清晰的 skill 形态。到 2026 年 4 月，具名游资 persona 仍然有热度和传播力，但最新市场信息也说明，单纯依赖人设包装会迅速滑向标签化、跟买心智和误导性理解。因此，这个产品机会不再只是把“人物热度”转换为“研究型 skill 单元”，而是要把 persona 入口转化为可验证证据、风险解释和责任边界都清楚的 research product。

如果现在只做零散内容页，后续随着更多游资席位与 persona 的加入，项目会迅速失去可维护性和可比较性。因此这次变更不仅要确定方向，还要一次性把 library 架构、证据分层、扩展规则、首批 persona 选择原则，以及 trust / provenance / risk rail 这几层定清楚。

## What Changes

- Create a persona-first A-share youzi skill library focused on named trader personas as the primary discovery surface.
- Reframe persona as the discovery entry while making evidence-backed seat behavior and risk explanation the primary product value.
- Add a standardized persona skill format that separates hard evidence, market interpretation, and uncertainty notes.
- Add a first-batch research workflow for building three initial persona skills with consistent structure and risk framing.
- Add an explicit library architecture that separates skill units, evidence materials, case materials, schemas, and indexes.
- Add an expansion-ready indexing model so new personas, seats, styles, and tags can be added without restructuring the project.
- Add explicit first-batch selection rules so the initial personas are chosen by heat, differentiation, evidence sufficiency, and controllable attribution risk.
- Add provenance cards, dual-lens output rules, and a responsibility layer so key claims remain traceable and balanced.
- Add persistent risk rails that position the library as research and education content rather than as a follow-trading or real-time recommendation surface.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `persona-skill-library`: Curated library for browsing and comparing named A-share youzi persona skills.
- `persona-skill-schema`: Standard skill structure that defines evidence layers, style summary, risk boundaries, and case-study sections.
- `persona-research-pipeline`: Research workflow for collecting, classifying, and validating public evidence before publishing a persona skill.
- `persona-library-indexing`: Index structures that organize skills by persona, associated seat, style tags, and future expansion metadata.
- `persona-claim-provenance`: Provenance model for tracking source tier, freshness, verifiability, and contradiction status of key claims.
- `persona-risk-rails`: Always-on risk and responsibility presentation layer attached to every persona skill.

### Modified Capabilities

## Impact

- Defines the product direction for the first implementation phase as a small, persona-first skill collection instead of a broad A-share cognition platform.
- Repositions the product from persona-led content to persona-entry, evidence-first research infrastructure.
- Introduces a reusable schema for skill content, evidence confidence, provenance, and boundary language.
- Defines the project as a scalable library rather than a one-off set of content pages.
- Affects future frontend content structure, research data organization, indexing strategy, trust/risk presentation, and publishing workflow for initial persona skills such as 陈小群、章盟主、炒股养家.
- Establishes the structural basis for future expansion to additional personas, seats, styles, and cross-persona comparison views without reworking the core model.

## Usage & Application Scenarios

### How this can be used
- **盘前风格判断**: Users can open a persona skill before the market opens to understand what kind of market environment, board behavior, or seat movement historically matches that persona’s style.
- **盘后复盘解释**: Users can review a persona skill after the close to interpret whether a day’s龙虎榜/题材/资金行为 resembles that persona’s known behavior pattern or is only being over-attributed by the market.
- **长期方法学习**: Users can compare multiple persona skills over time to build a structured understanding of different youzi styles instead of memorizing scattered community posts.
- **研究资料沉淀**: Researchers or content creators can use the library as a structured archive of persona notes, evidence, and cases.

### Where this can be used
- **Standalone research library site**: A browseable website where users read and compare persona skills.
- **Finance app research section**: A 龙虎榜 / persona / 复盘 module inside an app.
- **AI assistant workspace**: A persona-aware research assistant that answers questions using the persona library as structured context.
- **Internal analyst tool**: A private research knowledge base used by a small team before exposing anything to end users.

### What jobs this product can do
- Turn fragmented 龙虎榜、题材、席位、复盘 information into a consistent observation framework.
- Help users compare personas such as 陈小群、章盟主、炒股养家 using the same schema.
- Help users understand what a persona label likely means and where that interpretation may fail.
- Support future AI-assisted analysis by serving as a structured persona knowledge base.
